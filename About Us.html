Who We Are

A Legacy of Learning and Character Since 2001

Saint Augustin's School was established in 2001 by Former President J.A Kuffour with a vision to provide disciplined, high-quality education that prepares every learner for leadership and service.

Est. 2001 Founded by Former President J.A Kuffour

Our Story

From its founding in 2001, Saint Augustin's School has steadily grown from a small academic community into a respected institution known for discipline, academic consistency, and character formation. Each stage of our journey reflects a commitment to preparing students not only for examinations, but for meaningful impact in society.

2001

The Beginning

Established by Former President J.A Kuffour, the school opened with a clear mission to deliver disciplined, quality education grounded in moral values and national development.

2003

First Academic Milestone

The first graduating class recorded strong examination results, setting a high academic standard and building trust with families in the community.

2005

Campus Expansion

Additional classrooms and learning spaces were introduced to support increasing enrollment and create a more engaging teaching environment.

2008

Growing Community

Student enrollment and teacher capacity expanded, creating a vibrant learning community with stronger parent engagement.

2011

Leadership and Mentorship

Student leadership clubs and mentorship programs were strengthened to build confidence, communication skills, and responsibility.

2016

Academic Advancement

Modern learning practices, science activities, and leadership programs were introduced to support holistic student growth.

2019

Digital Learning Shift

The school adopted more technology-supported teaching methods, improving research, classroom interaction, and independent learning.

2023

Community Impact

Service and outreach activities deepened, helping students apply classroom values through projects that benefit the wider community.

Today

Future Focused

We continue to nurture confident, responsible learners prepared for tertiary education, entrepreneurship, and national development.

What Defines Us

Our Vision

To be a leading school recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and holistic student development.

Core Values
